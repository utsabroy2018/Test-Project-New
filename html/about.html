About Us

Julia's strength lies in the integration of interior and structure. Her trademark style is evident through the sophisticated use of texture, color, and light, and every home and office she designs receives her personal signature of timeless elegance and innovative simplicity.

Her distinguished designs can be seen in luxury homes and commercial spaces

Our experienced team has a comprehensive understanding of custom furnishings, antiques, textiles, and fine art. We specialize in high-end residential and commercial build-outs, sharing solid relationships with some of the industry’s most talented architects, contractors, and vendors.

We believe that homes and business should reflect the people who live, work, and play within.

From space planning to furniture and accessory procurement, our skilled team looks forward to working with you from conception to completion.

Prior to working in the design industry Julia worked in construction management in the accounting department where she spent twenty years effectively overseeing accounts payable and receivable, tracking rental equipment and expenses, and managing daily communication with vendors, customers, and bank representatives. her attention to detail and strong organizational skills ensure the effectiveness of completing a project where her wealth of knowledge and expertise in business and customer service makes her an asset to the completion of each project.
